Codechella FAQ

Programming platforms will be provided.

Students will write their codes at the event.

Yes. A team of eight students.

No prior experience is required or necessary. Competition details will be provided the day of the event.

No prior experience is required or necessary. Competition details will be provided the day of the event.

Competition details will be provided the day of the event.

Unfortunately, parents are not invited.
